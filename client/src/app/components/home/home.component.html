CAN YOU

Dig-It?

Dig-It was created for novice and professional gardeners alike who struggle to keep their plants strong and healthy. In short, Dig-It saves lives.

Some plants grow better in certain climates. Just plug in your ZIP code at registration, and Dig-It will give you growing tips that are specific to your location.

Dig-It Reminders notify you when you should plant your seeds, when your plants need to be watered, and even when you should harvest them!

Dig-It's extensive crowdsourced database allows users to get and share detailed information for any plant in the world!
